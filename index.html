Questions sellers actually ask.

These are the objections that matter if you want the page to convert with skeptical homeowners.

Do I need to repair or clean the house first?

No. The point of this model is to avoid having to fix everything before you can move forward.

How fast can this move?

That depends on title, condition, and your timing — but the process is designed to be simpler than a traditional listing.

How do you decide what to offer?

Condition, comps, repairs, carrying costs, and overall complexity. It’s a direct-sale model, not retail pricing theater.

What if I’m still comparing options?

Good. You should compare options. Better sellers make better decisions when they understand the tradeoffs.
